About

I'm a Lic. in Physics graduated from the Universidad Nacional de Rosario and now PhD Student in Geophysics at the Universidad Nacional de San Juan. I research forward and inverse gravity models at the Instituto Geofísico Sismológico Volponi with the support of a CONICET scholarship.

I'm also committed to contributing to Free Software projects to improve science openness and reproducibility.
