Leo has been awarded a Software Sustainability Institute Fellowship

Published on 2020/01/23 by Leonardo Uieda.

The Software Sustainability Institute offers yearly fellowships to fund the organization of communities around scientific software (creating of local user groups, workshops, hackathons, etc). Earlier this month, Leo was selected to be part of the 2020 cohort of SSI Fellows.

Read more about what this means and his plans for the fellowship in this blog post: Advancing research software in the UK through an SSI fellowship.
