Free online workshop on gravity data processing at Transform 2021

  • 23 March 2021
  • by Leonardo Uieda
    • meta

The 2021 edition of the Transform virtual conference is happening in 16-23 April 2021! This year, Santiago, Agustina Pesce, and Andrea Balza Morales are teaching workshop on gravity data processing in Python with our open-source libraries Harmonica and Boule.

Register for free (though donations are welcome) at: https://softwareunderground.org/events/transform-2021

Join us on the Software Underground slack for discussions and support during the tutorial. There are many more tutorials to choose from, a hackathon, unsessions, and more!
