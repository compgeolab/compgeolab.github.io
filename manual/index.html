Lab manual

The lab manual outlines our principles, expectations, guidelines, on-boarding process, and general information about how we operate as a research team.

About

This document is designed to be a reference for lab members and collaborators, explaining how we work and what are our expectations. It’s divided into two sections:

  • Handbook: Lays out our core principles and work ethic.
  • Guides: Explains of how we operate and other useful information.

The manual is a living document. Lab members are encouraged to submit fixes and improvements to it. See the Contributing Guide for this website to find out how.

Want to work with us? Before applying to join the lab or starting a collaboration, please read Open science and Authorship policy. These sections outline our core principles as scientists and it’s important that you are aware of and agree with these principles to a large extent. See Joining the lab for opportunities for students or postdoc.
